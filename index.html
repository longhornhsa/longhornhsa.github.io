About Me

Welcome to my website!

My name is Abhay Ram and I'm currently a Sophomore at UT Austin after growing up in the suburbs of Chicago. This website is mostly an avenue for me to showcase my creative endeavors and pursuits. I am a huge fan of digital media for its inherently collaborative nature and its ability to bring people together.
